About Archive of Our Own

A non-profit, non-commercial archive for transformative works

What is Archive of Our Own?

The Archive of Our Own (AO3) is a project of the Organization for Transformative Works (OTW). It is a non-profit, non-commercial archive created by fans, for fans, to preserve our fanworks and fan culture in accessible, non-commercial, and permanent forms.

Our Mission

The Organization for Transformative Works (OTW) is a nonprofit organization established by fans to serve the interests of fans by providing access to and preserving the history of fanworks and fan cultures in accessible, transformative, and legal contexts.

Key Features

No Ads

The Archive is entirely non-commercial and ad-free.

User Control

Creators maintain full control over their works.

Tagging System

Comprehensive tagging helps readers find what they want.

Open Doors

We preserve fanworks from sites that are closing down.

Accessibility

Features designed for screen readers and easy navigation.

Privacy

Privacy controls for both creators and readers.

Accessibility: Text-to-Speech

This mockup includes a built-in text-to-speech reader for chapter pages. Users can play, pause, adjust speed, adjust volume, and move across chapters while keeping focus on reading.
